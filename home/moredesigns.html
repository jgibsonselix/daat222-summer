Amid the murders of George Floyd, Breonna Taylor, Tony McDade, and countless others, I began using my art as activism.


Rise Philadelphia

A fellow classmate and I took to the streets to photograph the BLM protests in Philadelphia. From there, we designed and released a photo book, Rise Philadelphia.

Take a look!

Design to Divest (D2D)

I also connected with Black designers from around the country to develop a collective that is working to decolonize design practices, re-imagine design education, and provide free design services to socially-driven organizations.
